FAQ

Questions, answered.

How can your rates be cheaper than booking directly with SIXT?
We're a certified SIXT travel agent, so we book on the same corporate rate sheet as fleet partners — not the retail price you see on sixt.com. The lower price comes from the agent rate, plus the fact that we compare every branch in your city, not just the one closest to you.
What's actually included in the price?
Liability insurance, loss damage waiver, theft protection, unlimited mileage within the country, and a free additional driver. Taxes and airport fees are always shown up front before you book. The only things not included are fuel and optional extras like child seats, which you add at checkout.
Do I pay now or at pickup?
You pay at pickup, directly to SIXT. We never charge your card to hold a reservation. A small refundable deposit may apply at the counter depending on the vehicle category.
What if I need to cancel or change my reservation?
Free cancellation up to your scheduled pickup time. Changes (dates, branch, vehicle category) are free up to 24 hours before pickup. After that, we can still usually help — just call us.
What are the three rate tiers I'll see in the results?
Sixt Holiday is our lowest agent rate, ideal for leisure trips. Public Special is the standard retail-equivalent rate with full flexibility. Comfort SLI Incl. adds Supplemental Liability Insurance for higher coverage limits. We show all three side by side so you can pick what fits.
Why does the airport branch cost so much more for the same car?
Airport branches carry a concession fee paid to the airport, which can add 20–40% to the daily rate. Off-airport branches don't. The car is the same; the address is the difference.
How do I reach a real person if I have a question?
A licensed travel agent answers the phone 24/7 — including holidays. You'll find the number on your confirmation email and in your account. We also respond to email within an hour during business hours.
